In Western astrology, the Fire signs are Aries, Leo, and Sagittarius. In Chinese Wu Xing (Five Elements), Fire is one of the five cosmic forces — associated with passion, expansion, and the summer season.

But elements aren't just about your Sun sign. You can have **Fire energy** even if you're a Water sign — it depends on your full chart, and in BaZi, on your Day Master (the heavenly stem of your birth day).

Here are 5 signs you've got strong Fire energy — regardless of your zodiac label.

---

## 🔥 1. You get obsessed fast

When something excites you, it's *all* you can think about. You research, you plan, you tell everyone about it. New hobby, new interest, new person — you dive in headfirst.

This is the Fire element's natural enthusiasm. The Western Fire signs are known for this. In BaZi, it shows up as a **Fire Day Master (丙 Bing or 丁 Ding)** — people who burn bright and fast.

*Quick check: Born in a year ending in 6 or 7 (e.g. 1986, 1997)? That's a Fire year in Chinese astrology.*

---

## 🔥 2. You're impatient with "small talk"

You'd rather have a real conversation in 5 minutes than fake one for an hour. Surface-level interactions drain you. You're always looking for the *point*.

Fire doesn't hover. Fire acts. If you find yourself mentally checking out of polite conversation, your internal element is telling you something.

---

## 🔥 3. You lead — even when you don't mean to

In a group project, you're the one who ends up steering. At dinner, you're the one who picks the restaurant. You don't *need* to be in charge — it just naturally happens because you have presence.

This is classic Western Fire sign leadership. In Chinese elemental theory, strong Fire energy manifests as **visible influence** — people naturally gravitate toward you.

---

## 🔥 4. You feel things physically

When you're angry, your chest tightens. When you're excited, you can't sit still. When you're sad, it hits like a wave.

Fire processes emotion through the body. Western astrology calls this "Martian intensity" (for Aries) or "Solar pride" (for Leo). In Chinese medicine, the **Fire element governs the Heart** — emotional experiences are felt as physical sensations.

---

## 🔥 5. You've had at least one "burnout phase"

Fire burns. And sometimes, it burns out.

If you've crashed — physically, emotionally, creatively — you know what I'm talking about. High highs followed by a period where you can barely function.

**This is not a weakness.** It's a sign that your Fire element was running without balance. The counterpart is **Water** — rest, reflection, and recovery. Fire people who learn to rest become unstoppable.
