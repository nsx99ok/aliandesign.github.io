BaZi (八字, "Eight Characters") is the Chinese system of destiny analysis built from your birth year, month, day, and hour. Unlike Western astrology which maps the sky at your birth, BaZi maps **the elemental energy you're born into**.

Think of it as: **Western = your personality. BaZi = your elemental blueprint.**
